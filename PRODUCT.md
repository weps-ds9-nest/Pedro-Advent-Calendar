# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Internal team members evaluating tool capabilities and AI-driven automation.

## Product Purpose
An interactive web development advent calendar designed to showcase and evaluate agentic code-editing capabilities. Success is measured by guiding users through all 24 weeks of lessons using clear progression tracking and engaging, festive gating.

## Brand Personality
Retro, Playful, and Interactive. An 8-bit holiday theme featuring playful icons, punchy transitions, and retro font choices that evoke nostalgia and fun.

## Anti-references
- A basic corporate SaaS admin template (flat, boring, gray borders).
- Overly sleek, cold, or sterile modern corporate dashboards.

## Design Principles
1. **Retro Holiday Charm**: Embrace an 8-bit pixel-style aesthetic with bold borders and playful seasonal elements.
2. **Gamified Progression**: Use punchy transitions, feedback micro-animations, and retro icons to make unlocking doors feel rewarding.
3. **Interactive & High-Energy**: Keep interactions responsive, using dynamic hover states and retro visual cues to drive engagement.
4. **Focused Educational Content**: Ensure the core lesson material remains highly readable and legible, avoiding over-stimulating animations that block reading.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Support for Reduced Motion**: Provide the option to pause, hide, or disable snowfall and intense punchy transitions.
- **Contrast Compliance**: Maintain a legible contrast ratio for code blocks and lesson copy.
